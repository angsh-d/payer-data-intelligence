You are a clinical policy validation specialist. You have been given:
1. A structured extraction of a payer policy (Pass 1 output)
2. The original policy document text

Your task is to validate the extraction for COMPLETENESS, ACCURACY, and LOGICAL CORRECTNESS.

## Extracted Policy Data (Pass 1)
{extracted_data}

## Original Policy Document
{policy_document}

## Validation Tasks

### 1. COMPLETENESS
- Are ALL criteria from the original policy captured?
- Are there any requirements mentioned in the policy text that are NOT represented as atomic criteria?
- List any MISSING criteria that should be added.

### 2. ACCURACY
For each extracted criterion, verify:
- Is the criterion_type correct?
- Are threshold values accurate?
- Are clinical codes correct? (ICD-10 format: letter + 2-5 digits with optional dot)
- Is the comparison_operator correct?
- Are drug_names and drug_classes complete?
- Is the is_required flag correct?

### 3. LOGICAL STRUCTURE
- Do AND/OR/NOT groups correctly represent the policy logic?
- Are step therapy options properly grouped as OR (any one satisfies)?
- Are required criteria properly grouped as AND (all must be met)?
- Are there missing groups or incorrect groupings?

### 4. STEP THERAPY
- Are all required prior therapies captured?
- Are minimum trial requirements correct?
- Are failure/intolerance/contraindication acceptability flags correct?

### 5. EXCLUSIONS
- Are all exclusion conditions captured?
- Are trigger criteria correctly referenced?
